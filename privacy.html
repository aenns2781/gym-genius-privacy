Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how The Gym Genius ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application ("The Gym Genius").

Information We Collect

When you use The Gym Genius, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information, such as your email address and password
  • Profile information, such as age, gender, height, weight, and fitness goals
  • Workout data, including exercise history, performance metrics, and personalized settings
  • Subscription and payment information when you purchase a subscription
  • Usage data about how you interact with our app, including the features you use

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve The Gym Genius app
  • Create and maintain your account
  • Generate personalized workout recommendations
  • Process subscription payments and manage your subscription status
  • Send important notifications about your account or subscription
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our service

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers that help us operate our business, such as payment processors
  • Legal authorities when required by law

In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

The Gym Genius offers subscription services through Apple's In-App Purchase system. When you purchase a subscription:

  • Payment information is processed by Apple, not by us
  • Subscriptions automatically renew unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period
  • You can manage and cancel subscriptions in your Apple App Store settings

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Accessing your data
  • Correcting inaccurate data
  • Deleting your data
  • Restricting or objecting to processing

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

How to reach our support team

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the in-app support system in Settings.

To access the support system:

  1. Open The Gym Genius app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap on "Support" or "Contact Us"
  4. Submit your inquiry through the support system

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I delete my account?

You can request account deletion through the app's Settings > Privacy menu, or by contacting our support team through the in-app support system.

Is my workout data shared with third parties?

We do not sell or share your personal workout data with third parties except for the purposes stated in this Privacy Policy.

How long is my data retained?

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account, we'll delete or anonymize your data within 30 days.
